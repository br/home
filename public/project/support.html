Support

If you’re having trouble using Vagrant, want general advice on your usage of Vagrant, think you’ve found a bug, or just want to chat, the community surrounding Vagrant is ready and willing to help! You’ll find the various methods of getting support below.

  • #vagrant on Freenode for real-time chat
  • Google Group for a mailing list
  • Issue Tracker for reporting bugs
